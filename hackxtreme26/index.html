About HackXtreme'26

✦ HackXtreme'26 is a 24-hour hackathon that brings together developers, designers, and innovators to build impactful solutions.

✦ With the theme AI-Powered Innovation Across All Domains ,participants will use artificial intelligence to solve real-world problems across industries.

✦ Beyond competition, top teams gain internship opportunities with sponsoring companies, turning ideas into real-world impact.

✦ Build fast. Think smart. Create the future.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?+
Students from any college/university (undergraduate & postgraduate) are welcome. Teams of 3–4 members.
What do winners get?+
Winning teams receive prizes, recognition, and internship opportunities with sponsoring companies.
Is the event offline or online?+
Fully offline at K.Ramakrishnan College Of Engineering. This is an in-person experience — online participation is not available for this edition.
What should I bring?+
Laptop, charger,ID card and enthusiasm! Everything else — food, Wi-Fi, power strips, and sleeping arrangements — will be provided on campus.
What are the dates of HackXtreme’26?+
HackXtreme’26 will be held on April 9th – 10th, 2026, as a continuous 24-hour hackathon.
